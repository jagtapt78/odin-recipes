Salsa Recipe

This salsa recipe is really flexible, and can even be made boiled instead of roasted in the oven. You can add whatever ingredients you like to this basic mixture, like roasted pineapple, other kinds of peppers, mangos, peaches, avocado, or just enjoy it plain.

The most important components of this recipe fall into 3 groups: tomatoes, aliums, and aromatics. The ratios between these three groups is what is so flexible, but for best results, make sure you have the basics.

Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes (any kind)
  • Jalapenos, or spicier peppers
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Cilantro

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400F.
  2. Put all your ingredients (except the cilantro) in a cast iron pan or oven safe tray, then place them in the oven.
  3. After 15-20 minutes, remove your smaller ingredients, like chilies and garlic.
  4. Roast the rest of your ingredients for another 20 minutes, or until charred.
  5. Blend it all up!
  6. Serve with chips or on a burrito.
